Bauldur's Gate

Bauldurs Gate is a role playing game that loosely follows the rules of Dungeons and Dragons. You can play with up to three other people, and interact with many non-player characters. Including a dog that you can adopt. Save the Owl Bear Cub. Trust me.

Character Types

You can play as many different types of characters, warriors, theives, barabrian, palidin, or my favorite - a druid.

Game Play

As no surprise, when playing with my kids who had been playing for years, I made a few blunders. My kids have learned to save frequently, especially before battles. This was really important when I decided to try the only animal form I hadn't used yet mid battle with a bunch of goblins. It was a cat. I was imagining a big fighting cat, think thunder cats. That is not what I got.

Me: I'm going to try my last form, the cat!
kids:No! don't!!!

I became a fluffy house cat. With two hit points.

We all died.
